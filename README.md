# AikoFox AI Resume Checker

An AI-powered Resume Analysis and Resume Optimization platform that helps job seekers improve their resumes, identify missing keywords, and generate ATS-friendly resumes tailored to specific job descriptions.

## Live Demo

https://ai-resume-five-liard.vercel.app

---

## Overview

AikoFox AI Resume Checker is a full-stack web application designed to help candidates improve their chances of passing Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) and standing out to recruiters.

Users can upload a resume, paste a job description, and receive AI-generated insights including:

* ATS Match Score
* Missing Keywords
* Resume Strengths & Weaknesses
* Salary Estimates
* Interview Questions
* Personalized Resume Recommendations
* AI-Generated Resume Tailored to the Job
* DOCX Resume Export

---

## Features

### Resume Upload

Upload a PDF resume for analysis.

###  ATS Match Analysis

Receive an ATS compatibility score based on the target job description.

###  Missing Keyword Detection

Identify important keywords missing from the resume.

### Strengths & Weaknesses

Get actionable feedback on areas where the resume performs well and areas that need improvement.

###  Salary Estimation

Receive an estimated salary range based on the role and qualifications.

###  Interview Preparation

Generate personalized interview questions related to the target position.

### Personalized Resume Generator

Create a resume tailored specifically to the selected job description.

###  DOCX Export

Download the AI-generated resume as a Microsoft Word document.
